The problem

You know you need content. You just never write it.

The barrier isn't knowledge or motivation. It's format. Sitting down to write is a high-friction activity that busy business owners defer indefinitely. The blank page wins every time.

Meanwhile, your competitors are ranking for the keywords your customers are searching. Every week without a post is a week of visibility you're not building. Voice is natural. Writing is work. ContentCall removes writing from the equation entirely.

How it works

Four steps. Zero writing.

The only thing you do is answer a phone call. Everything else happens automatically.

1

We find your best keyword opportunity

ContentCall analyses your Google Search Console data to identify the query where a single well-written post could move you onto page one. Real data from your own site, not guesswork.

2

Your phone rings

At your chosen time, ContentCall calls your mobile. Our AI interviewer asks eight tailored questions drawn from the keyword opportunity. You just talk — naturally, the way you would to a client.

3

The transcript becomes a blog post

Your answers are transcribed, cleaned, and rewritten as a fully structured, SEO-optimised post — 1,200 to 1,500 words — in your voice, with your opinions, examples, and expertise throughout.

4

It lands in your WordPress as a draft

You get an email with a direct link to the draft. Read it over, make any edits you want, and hit publish. That's your week's content done.
